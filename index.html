About

This page is a personal archive dedicated to one of my favorite things in life: pizza. Over the past months, I’ve been documenting every pizza I’ve tried, capturing each one through photos and short notes. What started as a simple habit snapping a picture before taking the first bite slowly turned into a small project where I could track the different styles, flavors, and places that have shaped my pizza journey.

Inside this collection, you’ll find everything from classic Margheritas to experimental toppings, from homemade creations pulled fresh out of the oven to slices discovered in local restaurants and hidden spots. Each photograph represents more than just food; it’s a memory, an experience, and sometimes even a story worth sharing. Some pizzas were part of celebrations, others were late-night cravings, and a few were delightful surprises I didn’t expect to enjoy as much as I did.

As I continue discovering new slices, this page will keep growing. So feel free to check back periodically; you never know what kind of pizza might show up next.
